MGuest WiFi Network

MGuest is a wireless network service that provides basic internet access for use by University of Michigan guests. To access the network, review and agree to the terms of use.

Terms of Use

MGuest is a Wi-Fi network for University of Michigan guests. This network is allotted a limited share of network resources, does not provide encryption, is treated as an external network, and is available on a best effort basis. The university does not guarantee the privacy, confidentiality, or security of your communications while using this service. It is recommended not to use MGuest to send and receive personal or sensitive information. Certain U-M resources, such as library journals, are not accessible from this network.

MGuest is a part of the U-M IT environment. By using this resource, you agree to abide by the highest standards of responsibility. You are required to comply with all University policies, state, and federal laws concerning appropriate use of information technology. Non-compliance is considered a serious breach of community standards and may result in disciplinary or legal action.

For U-M students, faculty, staff, sponsored guests, and users from participating educational institutions, MWireless and eduroam deliver better performance, allows mobile devices to stay connected, provides encryption, and allows access to all U-M resources. The guest network is not intended for use by University faculty, staff, or students when engaging in university-related academic or business activities.

For assistance, please contact 4-HELP. Are you a member of the U-M community? Configure your device to use MWireless for full network access.
